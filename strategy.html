What's your strategy?

Having a vision is key to successful UX. By integrating my analytical mind and the knowledge I have acquired at the University of Michigan I have developed a vision and strategy for what makes successful UX.

A good strategist asks themselves these questions when embarking on any new UX project: Who are the stakeholders and what are their interests? What's the context and/or backstory? What are the necessary infrastrcutres? What are the requirements in view of stakeholders' interests? What are possible solutions to these requirements?

Have you asked yourself these questions lately?
